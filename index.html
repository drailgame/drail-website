Discover D:Rail

D:Rail is a versus racing game where the gameplay is focused on the throttle rather than on the direction. Prove your friends that you're the best pilot on our amazing circuits!

> Handle the throttle like a boss

In D:Rail, you control your car with the throttle on a very precise level. Push the pedal too much and you’ll get ejected from the track. Drive safe like a grandma and loose the race!

> Versus racing game

Play against a friend in a 1v1 or you can play with an AI. In either case, you better be the best or your pride will take a shot.

> Plenty of cars

Express your creativity by choosing the best cars with the best looks. Don’t worry, a shiny car won’t make you better, but you’ll shine like the star you are!

D:Rail
